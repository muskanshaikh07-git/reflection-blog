Reflection of presentation :- Group 4

I am writing the reflection on Group 4’s presentation which is on the “Experience of Women in Computer Science”. I think this topic is really interesting and it is also very important. The reason I think this is because it highlights the challenges and experiences faced by women in the field of computer science and technology.

I was really impressed by the presentation. It talked about issues like gender bias and lack of representation.The team gave examples that made me think about the struggles women face when working with men or studying in places where men’re in charge.The presentation team did a job explaining things in a simple way. This made the topic feel more real to me. I started to think about the difficulties many women have when they want to work with computers.The team helped me see that women who want to work in computer science face problems.The presentation made me realize how tough it is for women in this field. Not only this but the group also tried to spread awareness about the importance of equality and support for women in technology.

Learning about projects on communication information

In this unit we were introduced to two tools such as chatbot and dashboard which was a totally different experience compared to others projects. As these tools were totally new to me, I divided my work into small steps. Firstly I spent my time on exploring the chatbot and dashboard to understand their basic features. After this I mainly focused on planning about what I wanted to create and how the flow should be  instead of directly starting implementing. I look up for online tutorial especially here youtube helps a lot and side my side i was doing trial and error within the tools to learn how different functions work. The idea of breaking them into smaller steps really helped me to manage my time and also to reduce the confusion during the end stages.
