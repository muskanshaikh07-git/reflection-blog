About Me

Hello! I’m Muskan Shaikh, currently pursuing a degree in Information Science at Azim Premji University. I chose this field because I have a strong interest in robotics, especially the way it brings together software and hardware to solve real-world problems.

My journey into robotics started when I was in Grade 9. I got the opportunity to be part of a robotics organization, and that experience completely changed the way I looked at technology. I was introduced to different programming languages such as Java, Python, and HTML, and I also learned 3D modeling using Fusion 360.

What made the experience even more exciting was working in a team to build robots based on a game manual. From understanding the rules to designing, modeling, coding, and testing the robot, the entire process taught me valuable lessons in teamwork, problem-solving, logical thinking, and hands-on implementation. Seeing our robot perform tasks we had planned and coded was both challenging and rewarding.

These early experiences helped me realize that robotics is more than just an interest for me—it’s something I genuinely enjoy learning and experimenting with. Through this blog, I plan to share my learning journey, projects, and thoughts as I continue to explore areas related to technology, robotics, and software development.

Thank you!
