Reflection of presentation :- Group 4

May 11, 2026

I am writing the reflection on Group 4’s presentation which is on the “Experience of Women in Computer Science”. I think this topic is really interesting and it is also very important. The reason I think this is because it highlights the challenges and experiences faced by women in the field of computer science and technology.

I was really impressed by the presentation. It talked about issues like gender bias and lack of representation.The team gave examples that made me think about the struggles women face when working with men or studying in places where men’re in charge.The presentation team did a job explaining things in a simple way. This made the topic feel more real to me. I started to think about the difficulties many women have when they want to work with computers.The team helped me see that women who want to work in computer science face problems.The presentation made me realize how tough it is for women in this field. Not only this but the group also tried to spread awareness about the importance of equality and support for women in technology.

My thoughts mainly align with what was presented by this group. I think it is true that women often have to deal with things like stereotypes and not feeling confident because of what other people think. Women also do not get the opportunities as everyone else. The presentation says that if women get support from people and if they are encouraged and if they are, in a place where everyone is treated equally then women can do well in this field. Women need these things to succeed in this field. The presentation is talking about women and how women can succeed. I strongly believe that talent and skills should not be based on gender. 

Although, I also think that this presentation could have included examples of successful women in computer science to inspire the audience further. While the challenges were perfectly explained, adding the short stories of women on how they overcame this barrier could make the presentation more motivating. The one aspect is that the group could have done a better job in improving the visual presentation and organization of content and some points could have been explained more clearly and with better flow. 

Overall, I think group 4 presentation successfully created awareness about the experience of women in computer science and encouraged the audience to think about the need for equality in the technology field.
