My learning experience with binary and optimizing problems

March 25, 2026

In this unit, I learned about concepts that are related to binary numbers, decimal conversions, problem-solving (optimization problems) and transforming image. This all topics are new for me especially the topic related to binary numbers because as I change my major in second semester so I was unaware of what does binary numbers means firstly I consider this as normal numbers system. But later on I understood that computers only use 0s and 1s language which is known as binary number this concept was totally forgotten by me.

At the beginning, I found it a little bit confusing to understand how binary numbers work and how we are suppose to convert then into decimal numbers. However, after practicing with the help of my instructor in step by step methods , I started to understand the patterns and now I am able to connect this topic to real life as I learned that computers, mobile phones and digital systems all use the same language which is binary numbers.

Another topic which I found difficult was transforming image but later on it helped me to learn and understand how picture can be represented using numbers and how they can be changed or processed digitally. Optimization problems were also interesting because they required you to think carefully to find out the best and most efficient was to solve the different situations

The one challenge I faced was to understand how we should approach optimization problems but after learning this I understood that we can break them into smaller steps to make it easy to find the best solution. Practicing with different examples helped me to improve this skills.

Overall to me this unit was very much interesting as I learn many new concepts which I even never heard of it and also this concept help me to understand how we can use computing in real world application, Now I feel more confident and interested in learning more.
